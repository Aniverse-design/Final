We are C.O.A.L which stands for "Climate Organization Awareness League" and for over 10 years, we remain committed to saving the earth. At first we were a small team, but now we have almost 1,000,000 people who've been with us. we've been working tirelessly to save the earth from this catostrophic event that is currently taking place, We have motivated people from our team all around the world in almost every country who've taken the initiative to stand up against Global Climate change and we hope that you eventually become one of us.
